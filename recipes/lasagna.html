A Simple Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna can be considered one of the oldest types of pasta, it is usually rectangular or ribbon shaped made from a dough based on flour and eggs. Filled with meat.

Ingredients Required

  • 1 pack of lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • A clove of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • A pint of ricotta cheese

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil
  2. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes until al dente
  3. Preheat oven to 175 degrees Celsius
  4. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper
  5. Drain and stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic, simmer for 5 minutes
  6. In a medium bowl, combine the various cheeses and stir well
  7. On a pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled
  8. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, until cheese is melted
