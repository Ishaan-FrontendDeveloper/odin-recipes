A Refreshing Bhel Puri recipe

Description

This is a very popular street food in India, made with puffed rice, 'puri' which is like a crunch flavorless chip,boiled potatoes,onions, various chutneys,spices and sev (fried gram flour vermicelli).

Ingredients Required

  • 1 1/2 cup puffed rice
  • 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 4 chopped green chilli
  • 1/4 coriander powder
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 chopped coriander leaves
  • 2 tablespoon lime juice
  • 4 tablespoons roasted peanuts
  • 1 small chopped cucumber
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup boiled,peeled,cubed potato
  • 6 tablespoons tamarind chutney
  • 6 tablespoons green chutney
  • 1/2 cup sev
  • 1/2cup puri

Directions

  1. Take a large bowl and in it mix the puffed rice, tomatoes, onions and cubed potatoes
  2. Mix chopped green chillis, tamaraind and green chutney and add it to the bowl. Add black pepper to taste
  3. Lightly crush the puri and add it to the bowl. Add the remaining ingredients and mix well.
  4. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves,cucumber, roasted peanuts, ginger slices and lemon juice. Serve ASAP.
